How did freeCodeCamp get started?

Vicki started the Statalog community in early 2019. Statalog is the outcome of a necessity to find statistics and data as easily as a Wiki-like search. She is now just one of thousands of active contributors.

Can I create apps or tools based around the Statalog community and platform?

Yes. Statalog is open source (GPL-3 license), and data is publicly available. But you must make it clear that you don't represent Statalog itself, and that your project is not officially endorsed by Statalog.
